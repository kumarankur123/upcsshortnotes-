Key Bhakti Saints

1. Kabir (1398-1518)

  • Profession: Weaver PYQ!
  • Works: Sakhi, Sabad, Ramaini
  • Dialogue with Dharamdas: "Amarmul"
  • Philosophy: Hindu-Muslim unity, against rituals
  • Born: Varanasi (Kashi)

2. Guru Nanak (1469-1539)

  • Born: Nankana Sahib (Talwandi, Pakistan) PYQ!
  • Founded: Sikhism during Sikandar Lodi's reign
  • Philosophy: Monotheism, Nirguna Brahma, "God knows virtues, not caste"
  • Innovation: "Guru Ka Langar" (free community kitchen, no caste discrimination)
  • Death: 1539 at Derababa
  • Gurumukhi script: Started by Guru Angad (2nd Sikh Guru) PYQ!

3. Mirabai (1498-1557)

  • Born: Kudaki village, Merta, Rajasthan (Ratan Singh Rathore's daughter)
  • Husband: Bhojraj (Rana Sanga's son) PYQ!
  • Work: "Rag-Govind"
  • Devotion: Lord Krishna (after husband's death)
  • Contemporary: Tulsidas, Guru Nanak, Chaitanya

4. Tulsidas (1532-1623)

  • Contemporary: Akbar & Jahangir PYQ!
  • Famous work: "Ramcharitmanas" (in Awadhi language)
  • Other works: Gitavali, Kavitavali, Vinay Patrika
  • Based in: Banaras (Varanasi)
  • Mentioned in: Ain-i-Akbari by Abul Fazl

5. Chaitanya Mahaprabhu (1486-1534)

  • Born: Nadia district, West Bengal (Brahmin family)
  • Parents: Jagannath Mishra & Shachi Devi
  • Childhood name: Nimai
  • Sect: Vaishnava (Krishna devotee)
  • Lived: Vrindavan

6. Other Important Saints
