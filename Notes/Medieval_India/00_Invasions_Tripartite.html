⚔️ Chapter 0: Invasions & Early Medieval

Tripartite Struggle • Arabs • Turks • Rajputs

1. Tripartite Struggle (8th-10th Century)

Goal: Control of Kannauj (Symbol of sovereignty).

🦁 Palas (Bengal)

  • Founder: Gopala (Elected by people!).
  • Dharmapala: Founded Vikramshila University. PYQ
  • Patronage: Buddhism (Vajrayana).

🐎 Pratiharas (Gujarat/Rajasthan)

  • Founder: Nagabhatta I.
  • Greatest: Mihir Bhoja (Devotee of Vishnu/Adivaraha).
  • Traveller: Al-Masudi visited (called them "Al-Juzr").
🐘 Rashtrakutas (Deccan): The 3rd Power.
• Founder: Dantidurga.
• Krishna I: Built Kailash Temple (Ellora) (Monolithic). Super PYQ
• Amoghavarsha: Wrote "Kavirajamarga" (Kannada).

2. Arab Invasion (712 AD)

  • Commander: Muhammad bin Qasim.
  • Battle: Defeated Dahir (King of Sindh) at Battle of Rewar.
  • Source: "Chachnama" (Persian text). PYQ
  • Impact: Introduced Jizya for the first time in India. Introduced Date palm & Camel husbandry.

3. Turkish Invasions

🌪️ Mahmud of Ghazni (998-1030 AD)

  • Raids: 17 times! (Goal: Plunder, not rule).
  • 1025 AD: Somnath Temple raid (Gujarat). Ruler: Bhima I (Solanki). PYQ
  • Scholars with him:
    • Al-Biruni: Wrote "Kitab-ul-Hind" (Tahqiq-i-Hind). PYQ
    • Firdausi: Wrote "Shahnameh".
    • Utbi: Court historian ("Kitab-ul-Yamini").

🏹 Muhammad Ghori (1175-1206 AD)

  • Goal: Empire building.
  • 1178 AD: Defeated by Bhima II (Solanki) at Battle of Kayadara (Mt. Abu). First Defeat!
Battle Year Opponent Result
1st Tarain 1191 Prithviraj Chauhan Ghori Defeated.
2nd Tarain 1192 Prithviraj Chauhan Ghori Won. Founded Muslim rule in India.
Chandawar 1194 Jaichand (Gahadavala) Ghori Won. Jaichand killed. PYQ

4. Major Rajput Dynasties

🏰 Chandellas (Bundelkhand/Jejakabhukti)

  • Capital: Khajuraho / Mahoba.
  • Architecture: Khajuraho Temples (Nagara Style).
    • Kandariya Mahadev Temple (built by Dhanga/Vidyadhara). PYQ
  • Warriors: Alha & Udal (fought for Paramardi Dev vs Prithviraj).

👑 Gahadavalas (Kannauj)

  • Ruler: Jaichand.
  • Sanskrit Poet: Sriharsha (wrote "Naishadhiya Charitam").

⚔️ Chauhans (Chahamanas)

  • Ruler: Prithviraj III (Rai Pithora).
  • Poet: Chand Bardai (wrote "Prithviraj Raso"). PYQ

📚 Parmars (Malwa)

  • Raja Bhoj: "Scholar King".
    • Wrote books on medicine, astronomy, architecture ("Samarangana Sutradhara").
    • Built Bhojtal (Bhopal lake).
    • Built Sanskrit College (Bhojshala) at Dhar.
