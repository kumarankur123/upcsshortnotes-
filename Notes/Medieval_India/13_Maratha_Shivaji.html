Ashtapradhan - Council of 8 Ministers

"Ashtapradhan" was the Council of Eight Ministers constituted by Shivaji for Maratha administration Super PYQ Repeat!
Position Responsibilities
1. Peshwa Prime Minister of the King
2. Amatya/Mazumdar Finance & Revenue Minister
3. Waqianavis/Mantri Interior Minister (Intelligence & Espionage)
4. Sachiv/Surunavis Responsible for all correspondence
5. Sumant/Dabir Foreign Minister PYQ!
6. Senapati/Sar-i-Naubat (Sarnobat) Military administration, appointments, organization, food supply PYQ!
7. Pandit Rao Appointed for religious affairs (High Priest)
8. Nyayadhish Chief Justice
Important! Ashtapradhan was NOT a cabinet - each minister worked independently. Ministers acted as secretaries to Shivaji, not as decision-makers. Shivaji could accept or reject their advice.
