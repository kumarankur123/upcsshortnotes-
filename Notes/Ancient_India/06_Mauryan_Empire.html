2. Kautilya's Arthashastra (Political Bible)

  • Author: Chanakya = Kautilya = Vishnugupta (all same person).
  • Subject: Statecraft, Economics, Military Strategy, Diplomacy.
  • Compared to: Machiavelli's "The Prince" (pragmatic politics).

3. Bindusara (297-273 BC) - The Amitraghata

  • Title: "Amitraghata" (Slayer of Enemies).
  • Greek Title: "Amitragates" (by Greeks).
  • Conquests: Conquered Deccan region (between 2 seas - Bay & Arabian).
  • Greek Connection: Ant iochus I sent Deimachus as ambassador (replaced Megasthenes).
  • Son as Viceroy: Appointed young Ashoka as viceroy of Ujjain.

8. Third Buddhist Council (247 BC)

  • Place: Pataliputra.
  • Presided by: Moggaliputta Tissa (Buddhist monk).
  • Organized by: Ashoka.
  • Purpose: Settle disputes, compile Abhidhamma Pitaka.
  • Missionary Activity: Ashoka sent his son Mahendra & daughter Sanghamitra to Sri Lanka to spread Buddhism!

9. Decline of Mauryas

  • Last King: Brihadratha (weak ruler).
  • End: Killed by his own commander Pushyamitra Shunga in 184 BC.
  • Pushyamitra founded: Shunga Dynasty (184-75 BC).

üîª Reasons for Decline

  • Weak successors after Ashoka.
  • Financial drain due to massive welfare projects.
  • Ashoka's Ahimsa weakened military (debated).
  • Economic crisis + oppression of Brahmins (by Buddhist kings) ‚Üí Brahmin reaction.
