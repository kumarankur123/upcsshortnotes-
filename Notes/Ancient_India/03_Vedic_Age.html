2. The Vedic Library (High Yield)

"Veda" means Knowledge. "Arya" means Noble.

üìö The Four Vedas

  • 1. Rigveda (Oldest): Collection of Hymns (Suktas).
    • 3rd Mandal: Gayatri Mantra (Vishwamitra) - Dedicated to Savitri (Sun).
    • 7th Mandal: Dasharajnya War (Battle of 10 Kings) on river Parushni (Ravi). Sudas won.
    • 9th Mandal: Dedicated to Soma (God of Plants).
    • 10th Mandal: Purusha Sukta (Origin of 4 Varnas: Brahmin, Kshatriya, Vaishya, Shudra).
  • 2. Samaveda: Book of Music/Chants (Origins of Indian Music).
  • 3. Yajurveda: Book of Rituals/Sacrifices.
