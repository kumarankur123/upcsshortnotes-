🧱 Chapter 2: Indus Valley Civilization

The First Urbanization • Surgical Strike Notes

1. The Basics: Timeline & Geography

📅 Timeline

  • Mature Phase: 2300 BC - 1700 BC (C-14 Dating)
  • Contemporary Civilizations: Mesopotamia (Sumeria) & Egypt
  • Classification: Proto-Historic (Script exists but undeciphered)

🗺️ Geographical Extent (The Triangle)

Area: 13 Lakh sq km (Larger than Egypt + Mesopotamia combined!)
  • North: Manda (J&K) - Chenab River
  • South: Daimabad (Maharashtra) - Pravara River
  • East: Alamgirpur (UP) - Hindon River UP Special
  • West: Sutkagen Dor (Baluchistan) - Dasht River

2. The Twin Capitals

🏛️ Harappa (1921)

  • Location: Punjab (Pakistan), Left Bank of Ravi
  • Excavator: Daya Ram Sahni
  • Significance: First site discovered → "Harappan Civilization"
  • Findings:
    • 12 Granaries (2 rows of 6)
    • Cemetery R-37
    • Coffin Burial

🛁 Mohenjodaro (1922)

  • Location: Sindh (Pakistan), Right Bank of Indus
  • Meaning: "Mound of the Dead"
  • Excavator: Rakhal Das Banerji
  • Findings:
    • Great Bath (Ritual bathing)
    • Bronze Dancing Girl
    • Pashupati Seal (Proto-Shiva)
    • Bearded Priest (Steatite)
    • Woven Cotton (First in world!)

3. Other Important Cities

⚓ Lothal (Gujarat) - The Port City

  • River: Bhogava | Excavator: S.R. Rao
  • Key Feature: Artificial Dockyard (Port City) PYQ
  • Findings: Rice Husk, Fire Altars, Double Burial (Male+Female), Persian Seal
  • Mnemonic: "Lothal" sounds like "Boat" → Dockyard

🌾 Kalibangan (Rajasthan)

  • River: Ghaggar (Saraswati)
  • Meaning: "Black Bangles"
  • Excavator: B.B. Lal & B.K. Thapar
  • Findings: Ploughed Field (Earliest evidence), Fire Altars, Camel Bones

💧 Dholavira (Gujarat)

  • Location: Rann of Kutch | Excavator: R.S. Bisht
  • Unique Feature: Water Management (Dams & Reservoirs) PYQ
  • City Structure: 3-Part City - Citadel, Middle Town, Lower Town (ONLY site with 3 parts!)
  • Signboard: 10 large Harappan signs

🏭 Chanhudaro (Sindh)

  • Location: South of Mohenjodaro
  • Feature: Industrial Town (Bead making, Bangle making)
  • Unique: ONLY city WITHOUT a Citadel TRAP
  • Findings: Lipstick, Inkpot, Dog chasing Cat footprints

📏 Other Notable Sites

  • Rakhigarhi (Haryana): Largest Harappan Site in India
  • Surkotada (Gujarat): Horse Bones (Controversial), Pot Burial

4. 🦁 UP Special Sites (Memorize This!)

  • Alamgirpur (Meerut):
    • Easternmost site of IVC
    • River: Hindon (Yamuna tributary)
    • Findings: Pottery with peacock/squirrel impressions
  • Hulas (Saharanpur): Late Harappan site
  • Sonauli (Baghpat):
    • 125 Graves found
    • Evidence of Chariots (Warrior class)
    • North-South burial orientation
  • Mandi (Muzaffarnagar): Hoard of Jewellery found

5. Key Facts & "Trap" Zone

✅ What They KNEW

  • Metals: Copper, Bronze, Gold, Silver (First evidence in India) PYQ
  • Animals: Bull (Humped), Buffalo, Elephant, Rhino, Tiger, Dog
  • Script: Boustrophedon (Right to Left, then Left to Right) - Undeciphered

❌ What They DID NOT Know

  • TRAP Iron: Unknown (Came in Vedic age ~1000 BC)
  • TRAP Lion: Not known
  • TRAP Horse: Generally unknown (Surkotada bones controversial)
  • TRAP Cow on Seals: Cow was known but NOT depicted on seals

🕉️ Religion

  • Nature: Secular (No Temples found)
  • Worship: Mother Goddess, Pashupati (Proto-Shiva), Trees (Peepal), Animals

6. Origin & Decline Theories

Origin Theories

  • Indigenous Evolution (Amalananda Ghosh) → Most Accepted
  • Migration from Sumeria (Mackay) → Rejected

Decline Theories

  • Flood (Marshall)
  • Aryan Invasion (Mortimer Wheeler) → "Indra stands accused" PYQ
  • Climate Change (Stein)
