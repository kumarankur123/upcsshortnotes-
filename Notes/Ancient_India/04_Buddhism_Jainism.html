2. Philosophy (No Soul, Yes Rebirth)

  • Four Noble Truths (Arya Satya): 1. World is full of Sorrow (Dukkha). 2. Cause of Sorrow is Desire (Trishna). 3. Sorrow can be stopped (Nirvana). 4. Path to stop Sorrow (Eight Fold Path).
  • Eight Fold Path (Ashtangika Marg): Right View, Resolve, Speech, Action, Livelihood, Effort, Mindfulness, Concentration.
  • Pratityasamutpada: Theory of Dependent Origination (Cause & Effect).
  • Anatmavada: No Soul (Self).
  • Kshanikavada: Momentariness (Everything is temporary).
